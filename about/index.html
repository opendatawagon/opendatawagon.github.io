About Our Project

Vision

The vision for this project is to open up library data in order to:

  • Increase information sharing among libraries,
  • Expand funding opportunities for mobile services' programs by supplying additional data, and
  • Heighten mobile services' visibility, while also emphasizing ethics, accessibility, and protection of patron privacy.
